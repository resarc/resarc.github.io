In the Names of Thai series

There is the separation between Thai series, and it lies in the way of naming. On the one hand, series are being named in English and we can see that the series in this group has something in particular: their casts are comparatively younger. On the other, their names are in Thai and rather hard to translate into English, so they are generally use transliteration. The most obvious reason is their interest in reaching more varied audiences.
